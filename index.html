Survive as many waves as possible while earning points by destroying enemies, gaining boosts, and avoiding splices.

Enemies come in three types, each with thier own special characteristic. They each also have a weakness. If hit with a laser of the same energy type as themselves, an enemy will lose power. When they get to zero, they explode. However, if hit with the wrong energy type, they may gain power or splice.

When an enemy splices, it divides into more enemies. Depending on the energy type and strength of the enemy, it will divide into a number of new enemies with varying strength and energy types.
